I am primarily interested in teaching on topics related to international relations, international security, civil conflicts, natural disasters, natural resources, foreign aid, political violence, and research methodology.  

My teaching centeres around adapability to student needs in order to orient lecture and discussion around students' perspectives and needs in order to maximize their understanding. By spending time to approach concepts in ways accessible to students, I bridge learning styles and needs to tie concepts and arguments to contemporary issues, empirical analysis, and real-world application.
